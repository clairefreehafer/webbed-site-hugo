#### ingredients

- 1.5 oz silver/blanco tequila
- 0.5 oz mezcal
- 0.25 oz orange liqueur
- 0.25 oz ancho reyes
- 1 oz lime juice or muddled lime
- dash of choclate or mole bitters


#### instructions

1. mix all ingredients with ice.

2. shake and strain.

3. spritz saline over the top.
